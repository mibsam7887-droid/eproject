1. Personal Details

Band Name: Queen

Lead Vocalist: Freddie Mercury

Guitarist: Brian May

Drummer: Roger Taylor

Bassist: John Deacon

Origin: London, England

Formed: 1970

Genres: Rock, Hard Rock, Glam Rock, Arena Rock

2. Career Achievements

Over 300 million records sold worldwide

Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame (2001)

Multiple Brit Awards and Lifetime Achievement Awards

Famous for legendary performance at Live Aid (1985)

“Bohemian Rhapsody” considered one of the greatest rock songs ever recorded
